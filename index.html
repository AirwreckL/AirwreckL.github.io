Ender Storage

TEMP PLACEHOLDER

The #1 storage solution to storing items

MOD DESCRIPTION

Ender Storage is designed to have one block for the user to use, and functions just like a normal chest. However, it also allows for items to be searched up and taken out dynamically. This dramatically cuts down on the planning needed for item organization, while also allowing a balanced solution to the vanilla problem.

FEATURES

Two new blocks: The Storage Heart, and Obsidian Chests
The Storage Heart works like a regular chest, but also allows searching of the items you currenty have.
The Obsidian Chests are the actual storage parts of the mod, and contain the same amount of storage capacity as a regular chest. You cannot actually use these Obsidian Chests without the Storage Heart.
